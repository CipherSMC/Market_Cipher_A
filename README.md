# 📊 Market Cipher A: The Definitive Trading Guide

Market Cipher A is an "all-in-one" confirmation indicator. It combines multiple Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs) with momentum oscillators (WaveTrend and RSI) to provide high-probability entry and exit signals.

## 1. Core Components

### The EMA Ribbon
The "ribbon" consists of 8 EMAs. 
*   **Bullish Trend**: The ribbon turns **Blue** and expands. Price stays above the ribbon.
*   **Bearish Trend**: The ribbon turns **Gray/Red** and contracts or pushes price downward.

### Momentum Signals (The Symbols)
*   **Red Diamond 🔴**: A standard bearish momentum signal. It indicates that WaveTrend has crossed down in overbought territory.
*   **Blood Diamond 💎**: A high-conviction bearish signal. This appears when a Red Diamond coincides with a bearish EMA cross. It is one of the most famous "top-calling" signals.
*   **Blue Triangle 🟦**: A bullish continuation signal. It often appears when price is pulling back into a bullish ribbon before continuing upward.
*   **Yellow Cross (X) 🟨**: A potential bottoming or reversal signal. It appears when the market is extremely oversold, RSI is low, and Money Flow is beginning to curve up.
*   **Green/Red Circles 🟢/🔴**: These represent EMA crossovers (Long/Short EMA signals).

---

## 2. Trading Strategies

### 🟢 Bullish Entry (Long)
1.  **Trend Confirmation**: Wait for the EMA Ribbon to turn **Blue**.
2.  **Entry Signal**: Look for a **Blue Triangle** or a **Green Circle** while price is holding above the ribbon.
3.  **Confluence**: Ensure the Yellow Cross has recently appeared on the bottom, indicating a bottom has likely formed.
4.  **Stop Loss**: Place your stop loss below the most recent swing low or below the 8th EMA (the bottom of the ribbon).

### 🔴 Bearish Entry (Short)
1.  **Trend Confirmation**: Wait for the EMA Ribbon to turn **Gray/Red**.
2.  **Entry Signal**: The "Holy Grail" is the **Blood Diamond**. Alternatively, a standard **Red Diamond** or **Red Cross** near the top of the ribbon.
3.  **Confluence**: Price should be struggling to break above the ribbon.
4.  **Stop Loss**: Place your stop loss above the most recent swing high or above the top of the EMA ribbon.

## 4. Pro Tips for Better Accuracy

*   **Higher Timeframes Rule**: Signals on the 4H, Daily, and Weekly charts are significantly more reliable than the 1m or 5m charts.
*   **Don't Trade "Tight" Ribbons**: When the ribbon is thin and horizontal, the market is ranging. Cipher A works best in **trending** markets when the ribbon is wide and angled.
*   **The "Blood Diamond" Context**: A Blood Diamond appearing after a long uptrend is much more powerful than one appearing in the middle of a choppy range.
*   **Combine with Cipher B**: Traditionally, Cipher A is used to find entries while **Market Cipher B** (the oscillator) is used to find divergences. If you see a Blood Diamond on Cipher A and a Bearish Divergence on Cipher B, the probability of a move down is very high.
